Our History

Zenith started out as a group of friends refurbishing old shoes and reselling them as a way to help pay off their school fees. They did this using the money out of their own pockets, working from their own homes. Over time, they started gaining more popularity as people started spreading the word about their cheap, yet high quality products. Soon enough, the group of friends started their own little company using the money that they earned. They decided to branch out and become a reseller for popular brands, using their already extensive network of followers.


Today, Zenith has thousands of fans all over the world, all eager to get their hands on the latest and greatest kicks. Though we started small, we have grown into a large company providing shipping to almost all countries, all while preserving our values which helped us grow.
