Refund Policy

We accept returns and exchanges for up to 30 days from the day you received your order. All returned merchandise must be in new/original and resalable condition with all tags and original packaging attached. Please note final sale items cannot be returned or exchanged.

Print

Print the Return Note from the attachment of your confirmation email. Do state the reason for the return of item(s).

Pack

Pack your item(s) and attach the Return Note on your package.

Ship

Ship the item to our returns address.

*Please note: Return/exchange handling times may take longer due to the holiday period.

Terms and Conditions for Return/Exchange:

  • Selected items can be returned or exchanged within 30 days from the date you received your order.

  • All returned merchandise must be in NEW/ORIGINAL and resalable condition with all tags and original packaging attached. We will not accept any items that are not in this condition.

  • Return shipping fees are the responsibility of the customer. We will not provide return labels or refund shipping fees.

  • Please ensure all returned items are well packaged.Please make sure the shoebox is packed with an outer layer. As the shoebox is part of the product, we will not accept returned footwear with a damaged shoebox.

  • If you have requested for a refund, the original shipping fee of your order will not be refunded.

  • If you have requested for an exchange, the shipping fee for the new order will be waived. If we do not have stock for the size you requested for exchange, we will issue a refund to your original payment method.

  • If the item you received is INCORRECT, MISSING, or DEFECTIVE, please contact us within 7 days from the date you received your order. Please include your order number, photographs of the item(s), and all related references upon receiving your package.

  • Upon dispute, ZENITH reserves the right to make the final decision on all returns cases.
